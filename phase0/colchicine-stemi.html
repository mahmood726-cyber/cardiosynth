Quick Start

This dashboard queries ClinicalTrials.gov for colchicine + myocardial infarction trials with posted results, extracts structured data, validates each study through 10 TruthCert validators, then runs DerSimonian-Laird HKSJ random-effects meta-analysis on the primary outcome (MACE or closest composite).
